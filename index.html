Enterprise Imaging User Group

Sharing experiences.
Improving workflows.

The EI User Group was formed by customers of Agfa Healthcare using Enterprise Imaging and Xero Viewer.

We host meetings for members and run an online chat forum community to help each other, supported and endorsed by Agfa Healthcare UK.

Join User Group

Meetings

We hold 2 conference style meetings per annum, rotating the site each time to a different host member. Meetings are eligible for Continuing Professional Development (CPD/CME) points with The Royal College of Radiologists (RCR) and the Society of Radiographers (SOR).

Meeting format

Single day meeting with a mixture of EI User Group members, Agfa and other relevant vendors present

  • Demo systems to try out
  • Vendor lead roadmap lectures
  • Vendor lead in depth tutorials
  • Member lead lessons learned sessions
  • Tips and tricks - short workflow and configuration helpers
  • Feedback for feature requests and product improvement

Chat forum

Our spectrum.chat community allows the community to continue the debate between meetings. This is a closed community which you can apply to join, membership will be assessed by a moderator.
